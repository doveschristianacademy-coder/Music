🥁
Master the Beat First
Students and coaches should master the official STOMP · STOMP · CLAP · PAUSE rhythm together at 93 BPM. Stay locked in as one team — strong timing creates a powerful rally sound.
📖
Learn One Verse at a Time
Start with the Chorus — everyone learns "We will, we will rock you / D-C-A!" first. Then teach Verse 1, run chorus. Add Verse 2, chorus. Add Verse 3, final chorus.
📣
Call & Response Outro
Designate a hype leader (coach, student president, cheerleader) to shout the call. Crowd echoes the response. Practice the handoff: "Once a Cub —" → crowd: "ALWAYS A LION!"
🔊
Build the Final Chorus
Run the final chorus twice. First round at normal volume. Second round everyone goes all out. That crescendo is the peak moment — make it count.
✝️
The Story Arc
Verse 1 = The Cub (humble beginnings). Verse 2 = The Lion (identity claimed). Verse 3 = Lion of Judah (faith as foundation). Help students understand the journey.
🎤
Generate the Audio
Practice as a unified team during rallies, games, and assemblies. Coaches can assign student leaders to help keep the rhythm steady and the crowd engaged from beginning to end.
